Recent News (Scroll Right for More Articles)

14th September, 2249

Onwards, to Saturn!

After extensive lobbying, Leighton Logistics has acquired a permit to transport through the Saturn restricted trade zone. Deliveries will be available to and from a center on Eclipse Station on Enceladus from the 2nd of December.

21st August, 2249

Fuel Surcharge Increase

Due to recent increases in the price of liquclass hydrogen and oxygen, we have unfortunately had to revise our fuel charge rates. This will lead to a small increase in the price of shipping to all locations.

2nd May, 2249

Shipyard Blues

Several shipyards on Luna have been experiencing delays due to the recent protests. Because of this, the upcoming addition to the LL fleet, the UNS Race to Durango will be delayed for an unknown amount of time. This should not effect current shipping times or prices.

18th February, 2249

VAT Lowered

Due to the recent slashes in VAT taxes in the Mars Trade Zone, customers shipping from the MTZ will see a slight reduction in prices across all services. Enjoy the savings!

24th December, 2248

Drug Dangers

It is unfortunate that we have to reiterate this, but illegal drugs ARE NOT to be transported on Leighton Logistics ships. If these goods are found, the sender will be fined no less than 2500EM and will be reported to the local authorities.

18th November, 2248

Baseless Claims

It has come to our attention that several supposed ex-employees have been accusing Leighton Logistics of mistreating our workers and cutting costs by decreasing crew living standards on our ships. This information is blatantly false and should be ignored in its entirety.

4th August, 2248

Worry Not

Despite the recent attack by pirates on the Leighton Logistics vessel UNS Squaller during the Mars-Deimos circuit, Leighton Logistics would like to assure all customers that these attacks are infrequent and mostly harmless.

28th June, 2248

All Hail the Pioneer

Despite the setbacks due to the interplanetary raw materials shortage, the UNS Pioneer has completed construction and will take over the Europa-Ganymede and the Jupiter Research Station-Europa loops.

12th March, 2248

Resource Shortages

Due to the recent interplanetary resource shortages, the UNS Pioneer will be delayed another 10 weeks. We thank you for you patience.
